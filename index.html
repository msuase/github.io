Monica Likes Cats

Even though I do not have pets, I LOVE cats! For the most part they are low maintenance, and do not have the same in your face energy as dogs do. I think that anyone looking to own a pet that can give cuddles but does not need to be walked should get a cat. Adoption is a great way to give a cat a new home and achieve a forever friend! Since I do not have the means of being a cat owner yet, I encourage you to live out my dreams and get one yourself. I will be forever jealous!
